overview

purpose

the purpose of our site rafting project is to provide adventures of all levels with exhilarating and memorable whitewater rafting experience. we aim to connect individuals and group with the matural beauty of rivers while fostering a sense of adventure,camaraderie and respect for the environment.

audience

welcome to project "whitewater rafting" where adventure meet the river, get ready to experience the thrill of whitewater rafting like never before , our site is your gateway to adrenaline-punmping rapids,breathtaking scenery,and unforgettable memories.

question 1: a user wants to book a rafting trip for a group of 10 people. how would they go about it? answer: to book a rafting trip for a group of 10 people, the user would visit our website,s booking page, select the desired date and time for the trip, specify the number of participants(in this case,10) and proceed to checkout. they can also add any additional services or equipments they might need, such as wetsuit or helments, before completing the reservation.

question 2: what happens if there is inclement weather on the day of a scheduled rafting trip? answer: if there is inclement weather on the scheduled rafting trip, our website will notify customers via email or text message about any cancellations or changes to their booking. we prioritize safety above all else, so if weather conditions pose a risk to participants, we may need to reschedule the trip or offer refunds depending on the situation

question 3: how can users find information about the difficulty level of different rafting routes on the website? answer: users can find this information by navigating to the "trip" or "routes" section. each routes will be clearly labeled with the difficulty level, ranging from begonner-friendly to advanced. additionally, we provide detailed description of each routes, including any challenging sections or rapid classifications, to help users make informed decisions about which trip is right for them.
