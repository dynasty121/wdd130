history

In the heart of the rugged wilderness, nestled along the banks of a pristine river, the journey of our rafting company began over two decades ago. Founded by a group of passionate outdoor enthusiasts, the company started as a small operation with just a handful of rafts and a dream to share the exhilaration of whitewater Rafting.
